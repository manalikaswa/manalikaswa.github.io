## The Jack Ma Personality

- **Speaking style:** Easy to agree with, remember, and digest.
- A 'Reality Distortion Field' around him.
- Full of humour.
- Great ability to motivate people.
- Master at appealing to people's emotions.

## The Alibaba Business Wisdom

- **Local connect matters:** "eBay may be a shark in the ocean, but I am a crocodile in the Yangtze River. If we fight in the ocean, we lose, but if we fight in the river, we win."
- Long march >> running with short steps. It is not a battle, it is war.
- **Discussion forum:** Nobody comes to shop every day. We are more important as a community than as a marketplace — a non-revenue-generating entry point to the business community.
- He is always trying to understand how to get the money back later. He's just not greedy about getting the money first.
- Raise money when we don't need it. When we need it, don't go out to raise money — it's too late.
- As a business, if you cannot change the law, follow the law.
- **Ant as mascot:** the smallest creatures can prevail over their enemies provided they work closely together. The ants that unite can beat an elephant.
- **Social change:** entrepreneurs even funded the construction of the city's airport.

## The Alibaba Culture

- Customers first, employees second, and shareholders third.
- The mission is our religion. Messianic culture.
- **Cult:** Kungfu commerce with a dash of theater — mastery, discipline, and strategy with a touch of drama.
- Emphasis on camaraderie and a commitment to the greater good.
- Colleagues are friends and friends are family.
- **Company values:** the art of building up one's own internal strengths in order to defeat any opponent. The "Six Veins" of Alibaba's "Spirit Sword" are customer first, teamwork, embrace change, integrity, passion, and commitment.
- *Embrace change* is reflected in Alibaba's frequent rotation of its employees, switching them regularly between new products or between regions of the country.
- *Passion:* being a swordsman is all about being hot-blooded.
- Flat management hierarchy and minimized temptation to shame and blame.
- Employees are discouraged from ever complaining — a pet peeve of Jack's — and encouraged instead to shoulder personal responsibility, carrying out or delegating tasks rather than waiting for orders from on high.
- **Aliday** celebrates the team spirit that helped Alibaba overcome an unexpected challenge that tested its employees as never before.
- **Aliway**, the company's internal bulletin board: employees can even initiate polls or invite the support of their colleagues to dispute assessments or management decisions, and address suggestions or complaints.

## The Jack Ma Wisdom

- If there are nine rabbits on the ground and you want to catch one, just focus on one. Change your tactics if you need to, but don't change the rabbit… Get one first, put it in your pocket, and then catch the others.
- Today is brutal, tomorrow is more brutal, but the day after tomorrow is beautiful. However, the majority of people will die tomorrow night.
- The pioneers take the arrows, the settlers take the land (in contrast to being first).
- Masters of negotiation always listen, don't talk.
- Be the last man standing.
- First, the competitors didn't consider us a rival. Then they treated us too seriously as a rival. Neither was the right strategy.

## More Wisdom

- "Once you find out what you should do, then you need to stay focused. We resisted all kinds of temptations that could make money in the short term… That's how we got here." — Robin Li, CEO, Baidu
- "Business is like farming. If you only care about harvesting, but not fertilizing or cultivating, eventually the land will lose its vitality." — Zhou Hongyi, Chinese billionaire entrepreneur
- You have to go through a thunderstorm to see a rainbow.
